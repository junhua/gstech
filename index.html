Gilmour Space Technologies is an innovative company that is providing new and customized solutions for businesses, particularly in the aerospace industry.


Our key projects include the design and fabrication of static & motion-platform spacecraft simulators and replicas for space centres/museums, mid-to-large scale Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) for civilian and military applications, and a hybrid rocket program for suborbital passenger capsules and other payload systems.


We have a factory in Gold Coast, Australia and a representative office in Singapore. Our capabilities are to design, prototype, fabricate and build-to-order solutions for specific business needs.
