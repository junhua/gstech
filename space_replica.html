SPACE REPLICAS AND STIMULATORS

GS tech has designed and fabricated a number of replicas and simulators for our related company Gilmour Space Corporation to use in their Space Academy and Space Discovery center. These include static displays to full motion simulators of space planes and capsules. Fabrication techniques include welding, composite molding, 3D printing and painting; with materials including Steel, Aluminium, Fibreglass, Carbon Fiber, fabrics and plastics. We also have embedded electronics and mechatronics in our signature simulators. We have also supplied a version of our flight simulators to a regional museum and a local education facility.
