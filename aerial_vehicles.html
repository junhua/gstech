UNMANNED AERIAL VEHICLES

We have partnered with Singapore University of Technology and Design to design and build a series of prototype medium and heavy VTOL UAV vehicles. We have successfully tested a small scale UAV to transition from horizontal to vertical flight and back again. We are in the process of building a medium and heavy UAV with fixed wing VTOL capability. Our vehicles are primarily manufactured from composite materials. Our objective is to offer vehicles that can compete with existing medium to large scale UAV's with the addition of VTOL capability.
