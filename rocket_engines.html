HYBRID ROCKET ENGINES

In 2015, we began development of a Hybrid Rocket Engine Program to be used on suborbital passenger capsules or other space payload systems.


To date, we have tested a low-thrust evaluation motor using Hydrogen Peroxide and proprietary fuel waxes. We are developing a 5000 Lb thrust engine, with plans to further develop a 40,000 Lb thrust engine; and are working with Singapore University of Technology & Design to develop a prototype 3D printer for our solid fuel engine.
